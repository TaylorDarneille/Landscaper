Landscaping Game

Objective

The goal of this game is to earn $1000 while employing a team of starving students. Your teeth are your only tool to start, but you will have the opportunity to buy better tools the more money you make

Tools

  1. Teeth - cost $0, earn $1
  2. Rusty scissors - cost $5, earn $5
  3. Push mower - cost $25, earn $50
  4. Battery-powered mower - cost $250, earn $100
  5. Team of starving students - cost $500, earn $250
